Welcome
Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) was
established on 5 February 1964 in response to the
Defence needs of independent Nigeria to train
officers for the Armed Forces of Nigeria. Before then,
the institution was known as the Royal Military
Forces Training College (RMFTC). After independence
in 1960, it became known as the Nigerian Military
Training College. Read more
